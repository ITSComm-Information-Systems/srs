Instructions

Verify the purpose and action you want to take with each phone line and select one of the five migration options for each line:

  • Disconnect – Retire the line if no longer needed. This will end billing and service.
  • Migrate to Zoom Softphone – For an individual’s line.
  • Migrate to Zoom Deskphone – For an individual’s line.
  • Migrate to Zoom Deskphone (Common Area)
  • Keep Line Analog – For special services only (i.e., alarm, fax, modem).
Action Required: Complete all lines by Wednesday, March 25, 2026. Any unused or low-volume telephone lines that are not identified to be disconnected or migrated will be automatically disabled and moved into a maintenance hold. Lines in a maintenance hold cannot receive calls but will still be billed for.
